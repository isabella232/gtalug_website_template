GTALUG The Greater Toronto Linux User Group

Next Meeting: July 14, 2009

Kerberos, NTP, Samba and OpenLDAP as Active Directory Replacement: AD is so popular with corporations that you will be hard pressed to find any office without an AD providing centralized authentication. The group will discuss what AD is composed of, how to use the open source packages to setup basic AD functionality - the hard way, and finally how samba 4 promise to make this process less painful.
